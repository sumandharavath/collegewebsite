Our Facilities

Our college provides a diverse range of facilities, from sports and games to canteen and library, to cater to the varied needs of our students.

Library & Information Center

Our library is an excellent resource for students to conduct research and access reading materials. Our library staff are helpful and knowledgeable, providing assistance and guidance when needed.

Games & Sports

Our college has a well developed Basket Ball court, and several volley ball courts and Throw Ball courts and an internal Table Tennis Stadium

College Canteen

Our college canteen offers a wide variety of affordable and delicious food options. The canteen is clean, well-maintained, and provides a comfortable space for students to eat and socialize.

Campus Life

The campus looks sprawling with ample parking lots, shuttle buses ferrying students,
and a central administration block towering over the manicured lawns.

Transport

College operates 34 vehicles to provide bus facility to students from Hyder Nagar, Kukatpally and Hi-Tech city MMTS station.

ClassRoom

Classrooms in college provide a structured and interactive learning environment for students to engage with course material and collaborate with peers.

Seminar Hall

Attending college seminars can provide valuable insights, networking opportunities, and enhance overall learning experience.

About Us

Gokaraju Rangaraju Institute of Engineering and Technology (GRIET) is established in 1997 by Dr. G Gangaraju as a self-financed institute under
the aegis of Gokaraju Rangaraju Educational Society.GRIET is approved by AICTE, New Delhi, permanently affiliated to and autonomous
under JNTUH, Hyderabad. GRIET is committed to quality education and is known for its innovative teaching practices.
